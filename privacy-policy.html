Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025  |  Package: com.scrollcost.app

1. Overview

ScrollCost is an Android application that helps you understand the real cost of your screen time by converting app usage into a dollar value based on your personal hourly rate. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

ScrollCost is developed and operated by an individual developer based in Canada. We are committed to handling your personal information responsibly in accordance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and in a manner consistent with the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

The short version: We collect as little as possible. Your app usage data and personal settings never leave your device. We only receive what is strictly necessary to run authentication and subscription services.

2. Information We Collect

a) Information you provide directly

  • Phone number — collected when you sign in using phone-number authentication (OTP via Firebase). This is used solely to verify your identity.
  • Google account name and email address — collected only if you choose to sign in with Google (optional). This information is managed by Firebase Authentication and is used only for account identification.

b) Information generated automatically

  • Anonymous subscriber ID — RevenueCat assigns a randomly generated, anonymous identifier to manage your subscription status. This ID is not linked to your name or contact details.

c) Information that stays on your device only

  • App usage data — ScrollCost reads usage statistics from Android's UsageStatsManager (which apps you used and for how long). This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Hourly rate and app settings — the hourly dollar rate you configure and any other preferences are stored locally using on-device storage only.

d) What we do NOT collect

  • The content of any apps you use
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Device identifiers beyond what Firebase and RevenueCat require internally
  • Any personally identifiable usage statistics — usage data never leaves your device

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • Authentication — your phone number or Google account is used to create and maintain a secure account, so your subscription can be restored if you reinstall the app.
  • Subscription management — your anonymous RevenueCat subscriber ID is used to verify your subscription status and unlock premium features.
  • App functionality — usage data and settings are processed locally to calculate the cost of your screen time and display it within the app.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We do not use your data to build profiles for targeted advertising.

5. Data Storage & Security

On-device data (usage statistics, hourly rate, settings) is stored exclusively on your Android device using standard Android storage mechanisms. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party server.

Authentication data (phone number or Google account details) is stored and managed by Firebase Authentication on Google's infrastructure, which is protected by industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Subscription data is stored by RevenueCat. Only an anonymous subscriber ID and purchase receipt metadata are associated with your account.

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information we do hold. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. If you have reason to believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

We retain your authentication information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will request deletion of your data from Firebase Authentication. Subscription records held by RevenueCat are subject to RevenueCat's own retention practices.

6. Children's Privacy

ScrollCost is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction You may request that inaccurate or incomplete information be corrected.
  • Right to Deletion You may request that we delete your personal information. For authentication data, this means requesting deletion from Firebase. On-device data can be erased by uninstalling the app.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent Where we rely on your consent to process data, you may withdraw it at any time. Withdrawing consent may limit your ability to use certain features of the app.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint Canadian residents may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC) at priv.gc.ca. EEA/UK residents may lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We will respond within 30 days.
