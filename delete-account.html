Delete Your Account

You can request the permanent deletion of your ScrollCost account and all associated data by emailing us directly.

What will be deleted:

  • Your account and authentication credentials
  • All app usage history and daily records
  • Your settings and preferences
  • Any subscription records linked to your account

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
